Agentic AI Trade Intelligence Platform

The Agentic AI Trade Intelligence Platform is a sophisticated multi-agent system designed to automate the collection, processing, and retrieval of global trade data. By orchestrating LLM-driven workflows, the platform autonomously extracts actionable intelligence from massive datasets, reducing manual effort significantly.


Key Features & Workflow:

  • Modular Agent Architecture: Autonomous agents handle specific tasks such as web scraping, data standardization, and analytical reporting.
  • Advanced RAG capabilities: Integration with vector databases for seamless semantic retrieval of trade regulations and historical data.
  • Scalable Backend: Powered by structured database integrations and scalable backend architecture for seamless data flow.
  • Insight Generation: Provides analysts with deep insights, automated reporting, and natural language querying capabilities.
